Curry Udon Noodles

Return to Main Page

Description

Quick and delicious 15 minute curry udon noodle recipe.

Ingredients

  • Udon noodles
  • Japanese curry cubes
  • Cream
  • Broccoli
  • Garlic
  • Optional: Boneless chicken thighs
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Mince the garlic and chop up the broccoli

  2. Boil the noodles per the package's instructions

  3. If you would like chicken, cut up the chicken thighs and season with salt & pepper

  4. Air fry the chicken at 375 until the chicken reaches an internal temperature of 165 degrees farenheight. Chop up the chicken into bite-sized pieces

  5. Stir-fry the garlic until an aroma is in the air

  6. Make the cream by heating up some cream and one curry cube

  7. Add in the chicken, broccoli and noodles and stir until evenly coated
