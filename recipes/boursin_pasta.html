Boursin Cheese Pasta

Return to Main Page

Description

A nice twist on the famous tik tok feta pasta. The boursin cheese really makes it so much more creamy, and the best part, it's so easy to make!

Ingredients

  • Pasta
  • Boursin cheese
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Seasonings: Salt, pepper, oregano, chili flakes

Steps

  1. In a baking pan, combine cherry tomatoes, olive oil, salt, and pepper

  2. Bake for 30 minutes at 400 degrees

  3. Boil water, salt it, and put the pasta in

  4. Take the pan out of the oven and mix all ingredients together, smash the tomatoes a little bit if you like

  5. Combine the pasta with the cheese and tomato mixture and serve
